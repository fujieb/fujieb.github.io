Imua Ukulele Inc Identity & Collateral

A Hawaiian ukulele manufacturer IMUA Ukulele Inc., established 2012. A font for Imua and Ukulele head inlay were brought from client. Thin line logo for Elegant and high quality ukulele and ukulele shape for new ukulele brand. It also represents Imua's "i" for horizontal line. Several years later client changed more readable font for logo and ukulele head inlay to ukulele body shape. The brand identity is executed and carried through logo, tag, sign and T-shirt.
