BIOGRAPHY

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me.

Fujie A. Beutel was born and raised in Japan, and resides in Hawaii. Fujie received her Associate of Science degree in Interface Design from Kapiolani Community College from the New Media Arts program in December of 2009. Through her education, she has gained experience in graphic design, web design and coding. She is skilled in graphic design, including logos, posters, stationary packages, and web design, developing websites in HTML, CSS as well as Flash.


She also has working knowledge of information architecture and the development process. Fujie has worked in Japan doing handwritten lettering at Seiyu Discount Store, visual merchandising at Seibu Department Store, and organizing and managing design projects between designers and clients of advertising agencies at Adbrain K.K. In Hawaii, she has completed internships at Loomis-ISC and Nisioka & Associates. She is influenced by both Japanese and western concepts of design and aims to create designs that appeal and fuse both cultures.
