BIOGRAPHY

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact her.

Fujie A. Beutel was born and raised in Japan, and resides in Hawaii. She received her Associate of Science degree in New Media Arts, with a concentration in Interface Design, from Kapiolani Community College in December of 2009. She is skilled in graphic design, including logos, posters, and stationary, as well as web design, developing websites in HTML and CSS.


Fujie also has working knowledge of information architecture and the web development process. She has completed internships at Loomis-ISC and Nisioka & Associates. She has made websites and graphic designs for Jacy-Inc since 2010, as well as making web and graphic design for NDPTC since 2011. She is influenced by both Japanese and western concepts of design, and aims to create designs that appeal and fuse both cultures.
